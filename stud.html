Urcuchillay Lorenzo

Champion Huacaya Stud – Fawn

Urcuchillay Lorenzo is a quality fawn Huacaya stud combining strong genetics with excellent fleece characteristics. He carries bloodlines known for producing alpacas with fine fibre, density and good overall conformation.

He displays a well-structured fleece with a soft handle, consistent crimp and good staple definition, along with strong frame and coverage. Lorenzo has already proven himself as a productive sire, passing on his fleece quality, uniformity and colour to the next generation.

His offspring have demonstrated real promise in the show ring, reflecting the strength of his pedigree and the consistency of his traits.

Urcuchilleay Lorenzo offers breeders the opportunity to introduce reliable fawn genetics while maintaining the fleece quality and structure essential for a progressive breeding programme.

Show Highlights

  • Champion – Best Light Male (North Somerset Show 2018)
  • Champion – Best Beige Huacaya Fleece (Somerset Fleece Show 2019)
  • Reserve Champion – Light Male (Somerset County Show 2019)
  • Multiple First Place Class Wins

Hushabye Farm Cassius Clay

All Ireland & Northern Ireland Champion Huacaya Stud – Black

Hushabye Farm Cassius Clay is a striking black Huacaya stud combining powerful presence with exceptional fleece characteristics. Bred by the well-known Hushabye breeding programme, Cassius Clay brings proven black genetics alongside the fleece quality and conformation breeders seek when strengthening colour lines.

Cassius Clay carries a dense, well-structured fleece with a soft handle, consistent crimp style and excellent staple definition. His fleece shows strong coverage and uniformity across the body, supported by a balanced frame and solid conformation.

His quality has been recognised in the show ring where he has achieved All Ireland Champion and Northern Ireland Champion titles, confirming his outstanding fleece and overall presence.

Now proving himself as a productive sire, Cassius Clay is passing on colour consistency, fleece density and good structure to his offspring, making him a valuable addition to any progressive breeding programme.

Show Highlights

  • Champion – Black Huacaya (Southern AAI Show 2018)
  • Champion – Black Huacaya (NI Alpaca Group Show 2019)
  • 1st Black Adult/Senior Male (NI Alpaca Group Show 2019)
