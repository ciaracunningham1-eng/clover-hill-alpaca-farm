Stud Services

We offer high quality stud services from carefully selected award-winning Huacaya males known for excellent fleece quality, strong conformation and calm temperament.

Our carefully selected breeding males come from proven bloodlines and award-winning genetics. Please contact us for further details about availability and breeding arrangements.

Cassius Clay

All Ireland Show Champion | Northern Ireland Show Champion.

Cassius Clay is a striking solid black Huacaya male who has proven himself in both the Southern AAI and Northern NIAG show circuits, demonstrating excellent conformation, strong presence and outstanding fleece quality.

Cassius comes from impressive award-winning genetics, being the grandson of Lillyfield Jack of Spades and Popham Thunder.

He carries a very fine, dense fleece combined with a well-balanced frame and an attractive head style. As a proven herd sire, Cassius consistently passes on his strong conformation, colour and quality fleece to his cria, many of which inherit his distinctive teddy-bear head, fluffy ears and calm temperament.

Urcuchillay Lorenzo

Champion Huacaya Stud – Fawn

Urcuchillay Lorenzo is a multiple award-winning Huacaya stud combining proven genetics with excellent fleece characteristics. Bred by the respected Urcuchillay breeding programme, Lorenzo displays the traits breeders seek when improving fleece quality and overall herd performance.

His fleece shows strong density, consistent crimp style and a soft, uniform handle, supported by excellent staple length and coverage across the body.

Show Highlights

  • Champion – Best Beige Huacaya Fleece (Somerset Fleece Show 2019)
  • Reserve Champion – Light Male (Somerset County Show 2019)
  • Multiple First Place Class Wins
